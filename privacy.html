Privacy Policy for Pavlet EQ

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pavlet EQ ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the app. We built Pavlet EQ to work without collecting personal data from you, and this policy describes exactly what that means in practice.

1. Information we do not collect

Pavlet EQ does not require you to create an account, does not collect your name, email address, or contact details, and does not use analytics or advertising tracking software. The app does not access your contacts, photos, location, or microphone beyond what is strictly needed for its core audio features to function on your device.

2. Information processed for purchases

Pavlet EQ offers optional premium features through in-app purchases (a yearly subscription and a one-time lifetime purchase). These purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store, and purchase validation is handled by our subscription infrastructure provider, RevenueCat.

When you make a purchase, Apple and RevenueCat may process:

  • An anonymous, randomly generated identifier used to recognize your purchase across app launches (not linked to your name or Apple ID)
  • Transaction and receipt data necessary to verify your purchase and grant access to premium features
  • Your device's App Store country/region, used only to display the correct local pricing

We do not receive your payment details, card number, or Apple ID directly — these are handled entirely within Apple's own systems in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy.

3. Data stored on your device

Any settings, presets, or preferences you configure within Pavlet EQ are stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to us or any third party, and is removed if you delete the app.

4. Third-party services

Pavlet EQ uses the following third-party service solely to manage in-app purchases:

  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — payment processing. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or crash reporting tools that collect personal data at this time. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

5. Data retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to retain or delete on request. Purchase records are retained by Apple and RevenueCat according to their own retention policies, for the purpose of maintaining your access to purchased features and for legal/accounting requirements.

6. Children's privacy

Pavlet EQ does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal data from any user, this applies equally to users of all ages.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
